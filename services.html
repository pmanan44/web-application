Our Services

Animal Education

To get a job as an animal control officer in Ontario, you must be certified by the Ontario Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals, or OSPCA for short. The rules differ from province to province, and Ontario's rules are relatively straightforward.

Wildlife Conservation

Wildlife conservation is the preservation and protection of animals, plants, and their habitats. By conserving wildlife, we're ensuring that future generations can enjoy our natural world and the incredible species that live within it.

Animal Welfare

The Provincial Animal Welfare Services Act covers offences, including: causing or permitting distress to an animal. causing harm or attempting to cause harm to a law enforcement or service animal. promoting, arranging or receiving a financial benefit from animal fighting events.

Knowledge About Animals

We provide the best knowledge about animals like tigers, horses, dogs, cats, and cows. There are almost 1.2 million species alive on earth.

Animal Rescue

We provide rescue services and advocate for the protection and conservation of wild animals. Our operations include rescue missions and rehabilitation efforts.

Wildlife Protection

We work with government agencies to implement wildlife protection rules and combat illegal activities that threaten animal populations.
