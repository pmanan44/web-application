Dog

Dogs are known for their loyalty and social nature. They exhibit a wide range of behaviors, from playful antics to protective instincts. Dogs vary greatly in size, from tiny Chihuahuas to giant Great Danes. They have an acute sense of smell and are often trained for tasks like hunting, herding, and assisting people with disabilities.

Cat

Cats have nearly twice the amount of neurons in their cerebral cortex as dogs. Cats have the largest eyes relative to their head size of any mammal. Cats make very little noise when they walk around. The thick, soft pads on their paws allow them to sneak up on their prey.

Horse

Horses are majestic creatures known for their strength, speed, and beauty. They have been domesticated for thousands of years and are used for various purposes, including riding, racing, and agricultural work. Horses come in different breeds, each with unique characteristics and abilities. They require proper care, including feeding, grooming, and exercise, to maintain their health and well-being.
